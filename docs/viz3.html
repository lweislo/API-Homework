Absolute Latitude vs. Humidity

Humidity Slightly Correlated to Latitude

  • There was a slight correlation between latitude and humidity when looking only at cities that were in darkness at the time of measurement.
  • There was no significant correlation when cities were in daylight.
  • This difference was not detected in a separate dataset taken at a different time (not shown).
